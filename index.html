ABOUT US

McLord Cooling & Events is a proudly Nigerian
event company dedicated to delivering comfort,
style, and refreshment to every celebration.
We provide chilled drinks using mobile cooling vans,
and also offer full event planning and support services.
From weddings, birthdays, and burials to school events,
religious gatherings, and corporate functions —
we ensure your event is well-coordinated and unforgettable.
With a reputation for reliability and friendly service,
McLord is your trusted partner for cold drinks,
cool vibes, and successful events.
